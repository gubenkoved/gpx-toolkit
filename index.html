GPX Toolkit

Welcome! GPX Toolkit is one home for all your rides. Connect a source — your Beeline account or your own GPX files — and they live together in one library you can explore, map, analyze and push to Strava. You can add or change sources any time from Sources in the header.

Connect the sources your rides come from. They all live together in one library.

Beeline Velo account
Sign in to your Beeline Velo account to download your whole ride history at once — routes, stats and Strava status — with fast uploads and no cable.
GPX files
Import rides from .gpx files (or a .zip bundle) recorded by any device or app — explore, map and analyze them alongside your Beeline rides. No account needed.
